Privacy Policy

Data Collection

The application does not require registration nor collects personally identifiable data (such as name, email, or phone number).

  • Camera: Only for scanning QR codes.
  • Contacts and calendar: only if the user wants to generate a QR with this information.
  • Privacy: in no case are these data stored or shared outside the device.

Third-party Services

  • Firebase Analytics: Used anonymously to understand how the application is used and improve its performance.
  • Google AdMob: Used to display ads. It may use device identifiers to personalize advertising, always with user consent.

Information Protection

Sensitive data is not stored or transmitted. All information generated in QR codes is kept on the user's device.

Minors

The application is designed for general use and does not intentionally collect information from minors.

Changes to the Policy

This policy may be updated at any time. It is recommended to review this section periodically.
