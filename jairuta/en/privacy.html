Introduction

Welcome to JaiRoute, a route optimization application developed by Emelagudev. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection when you use our mobile application.

Key Point: JaiRoute is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect personally identifiable information, and our app works without requiring user registration or login.

Information We Collect

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • User accounts or profiles
  • Contact lists or personal contacts
  • Photos, videos, or other media files

Information We DO Collect

  • Anonymous Usage Analytics: Through Firebase Analytics, we collect anonymous data about how you interact with the app (screens visited, features used, app crashes)
  • Device Information: Basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and performance optimization
  • Route Optimization Data: We may collect user and/or stop coordinates to improve route optimization algorithms and provide better routing suggestions

How We Use Your Information

The limited anonymous data we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Identifying and fixing bugs or technical issues
  • Understanding which features are most valuable to users
  • Making informed decisions about future app development

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising targeting, or any commercial purposes beyond app improvement.

Third-Party Services

Firebase Analytics (Google)

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics. This service helps us understand app performance and user behavior patterns without identifying individual users.

Google AdMob

JaiRoute displays advertisements through Google AdMob to keep the app free for all users. AdMob may collect certain information for ad serving purposes:

  • Device advertising ID (which you can reset in your device settings)
  • General location information (country/region level only)
  • App usage patterns for ad relevance

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings.

Mapping Services

Route calculations may utilize third-party mapping and routing services. These services process route requests temporarily and do not store your specific route information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect the limited data we collect:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Anonymous analytics data is stored securely on Google's Firebase servers
  • We do not store sensitive personal information that could identify you
  • Regular security assessments ensure our practices remain current

Your Rights and Choices

  • Analytics Opt-out: You can disable analytics tracking through your device's privacy settings
  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's advertising settings
  • App Permissions: You can review and modify app permissions in your device settings at any time
  • Data Deletion: Since we don't collect personal data, there's no personal data to delete. Anonymous analytics cannot be traced back to individual users

Children's Privacy

JaiRoute is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since our app doesn't collect personally identifiable information from any users, it complies with children's privacy protection requirements.

International Users

JaiRoute is available worldwide. The anonymous data we collect may be processed in various countries where our service providers operate, including the United States. We ensure that all data processing complies with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. When we make significant changes, we will notify users through the app or update the "Last Updated" date below. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
